About Me

I guess the most common story to why I wanted to study computer science in college was my fascination with computers and the internet. I have the most vivid memories of using google and microsoft powerpoint for the first time on bulky box computers. That is why I'm grateful to my third grade teacher for introducing me to computers. I was excited to explore what the internet and computing had to offer, but I was also interested in what the future of technology was. I never played a lot of video games until middle school when I got my first xbox. Gaming became one of my hobbies. I believe in the idea of having three hobbies.
